About Me

Front-end developer from Bulawayo, Zimbabwe

My name is Moud Nkazimulo Mpofu, and I was born and raised in Bulawayo, Zimbabwe. I moved to Poland, Europe in 2017 to study Civil and Environmental Engineering.


During high school, I participated in chess competitions at the national level. Currently, I enjoy painting as a means of unwinding and detaching from the outside world. Additionally, I play the guitar.

Since high school, I have been keen on learning coding and had the opportunity to join Zimcode, a program that teaches high scholars in Zimbabwe, Bulawayo, to code. During this program, I learned the basics of Python and enjoyed building applications using it. Ever since, I have been searching for an online program that could provide me with a comprehensive understanding of coding, which led me to SheCodes. As an online course that teaches coding to women worldwide, my experience with SheCodes has been nothing short of amazing so far.
